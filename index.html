Hey, I'm Jea 👋

I'm a BSIT graduate based in the Philippines with a deep passion for UI/UX Design and graphic design. I love turning ideas into intuitive, beautiful digital experiences — from brand identities that tell a story to interfaces that feel effortless. I believe great design is invisible — it just works, and it makes you feel something.

I've been building my craft across graphic design, brand identity, and UI/UX through school projects, freelance work, and internships — and I'm just getting started. Every poster, every logo, every interface is a chance to grow. I show up, I learn fast, and I bring heart to everything I create.

My goal? To be a designer who builds visual systems that truly help people — from brand identities to product interfaces. I'm drawn to design that communicates, not just decorates — and I'm investing every day into getting better. If you're looking for someone hungry to learn and ready to create.
