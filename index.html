I love turning ideas into intuitive, beautiful digital experiences — from brand identities that tell a story to interfaces that feel effortless. I believe great design is invisible — it just works, and it makes you feel something. I've been building my craft across **graphic design, brand identity, and UI/UX** through school projects, freelance work, and internships — and I'm just getting started. To be a **designer who builds visual systems that truly help people** — from brand identities to product interfaces. I'm drawn to design that *communicates*, not just decorates — and I'm investing every day into getting better. If you're looking for someone hungry to learn and ready to create.
