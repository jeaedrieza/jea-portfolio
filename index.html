Hey, I'm Jea 👋

I'm a BSIT graduate based in the Philippines with a deep passion for UI/UX Design and visual storytelling. I love turning ideas into intuitive, beautiful digital experiences — from interfaces that feel effortless to videos that move people. I believe great design is invisible — it just works, and it makes you feel something.

I've been exploring graphic design, video editing, and brand identity through school projects and personal work — and I'm just getting started. Every poster, every edit, every logo is a chance to grow. I'm not where I want to be yet, but I show up, I learn fast, and I bring heart to everything I create.

My goal? To become a UI/UX Designer who builds things that truly help people. I'm drawn to design that communicates, not just decorates — and I'm investing every day into getting better. If you're looking for someone hungry to learn and ready to create.
